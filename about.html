About Blockchain

A secure, decentralized ledger that transforms how finance and trust work online.

What is Blockchain?

Blockchain stores records in chained blocks so information cannot be altered after it is validated.

How it Works

Transactions are broadcast to a distributed network and added only after consensus is reached.

Why it Matters

It reduces fraud, speeds settlement, and creates digital trust for Indian financial systems.
